About Me

I am a brewing student at the University of Maine! My favorite brews are stouts, but I'll enjoy any craft beer. My favorite brewery is Marsh Island Brewing in Orono, Maine

My favorite commercial beers are: Guiness (Stout), Yuengling (Pilsner), Michelob Ultra (Light Lager ), and Modelo (Mexican Lager).When I drink wine I prefer a Cabernet Sauvignon or a Malbec.

When I am not studying Brewing, or trying new beers, I study computer science. I am a senior computer science student with a passion for DevOps and Cybersecurity.

Beer is not the only beverage I enjoy, I also love wine. I prefer to enjoy alcohol responsibly, and would encourage you as well. If you would like to talk about fine wine, software design, or plan a beer tasting time, you can email me at sean.radel@maine.edu
